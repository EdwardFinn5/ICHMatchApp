ICF Connect

Iowa College Foundation's Internship/Job Portal

ICF Connect is an internship/job portal created to provide Iowa companies and organizations a way in which to connect with the 32,000+ students attending Iowa College Foundation's member schools -- 23 private colleges and universities in Iowa.
